Cyber Sleuth

Organized by Cybernerds KARE

An intensive cybersecurity competition designed to challenge and enhance the skills of CSE, IT, and CS/IT students. The event simulates real-world cyber threat scenarios, pushing participants to apply theoretical knowledge in practical, high-pressure situations.

Coding Combat

A debugging challenge that assesses participants' ability to identify and fix vulnerabilities in code.

Brain Battle

A quiz-style round that tests contestants' theoretical knowledge across various cybersecurity domains.

Threat Intelligence

A one-hour Capture The Flag (CTF) competition that simulates a real-world cyber threat scenario.
